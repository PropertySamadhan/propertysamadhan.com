Our Trusted Real Estate
1990s

Humble Beginnings

In 1992, Hari Ram Ji Agrawal laid the foundation for what would later become a trusted name in Indore’s real estate industry. Under the banner of Agrawal Constructions, the firm started as a small builder-ship and marketing company, dedicated to helping people find their dream homes. With integrity and hard work at its core, the firm slowly built a reputation for quality and reliability. Meanwhile, a young Kapil Agrawal was growing up watching his father’s dedication to the business. Inspired by his father’s principles of trust and customer satisfaction, he knew that real estate was his calling. At just 17 years old, Kapil Agrawal joined the family business.

Our Trusted Real Estate
2000s

A Young Visionary Steps In

Kapil Agrawal determined to carry forward his father’s legacy of trust and excellence. It was a time of rapid change in Indore’s real estate market, and Kapil quickly adapted, learning the intricacies of the trade. Through his unwavering commitment, he strengthened the firm’s relationships with clients, builders, and investors, earning the trust of the community. He didn’t just sell properties—he helped people build their futures.

Our Trusted Real Estate
2010

A New Direction

By the late 2010s, Kapil Agrawal took a bold step. Recognizing the shifting landscape of real estate, he decided to move away from builder-ship and focus entirely on marketing and brokerage. This led to the birth of Agrawal Realtors, a firm solely dedicated to connecting buyers and sellers with the best investment opportunities. Through strategic marketing, in-depth market knowledge, and a client-first approach, Agrawal Realtors became a well-respected name in Indore’s real estate market. The firm successfully marketed numerous residential and commercial projects, helping countless families find their dream homes.

Our Trusted Real Estate
2020

The Evolution to Agrawal & Makwana Realtors

As the business continued to grow, Rajesh Makwana joined forces with Kapil Agrawal in early 2020, bringing with him exceptional marketing skills and team-building expertise. With this partnership, the firm underwent a major transformation and was rebranded as Agrawal & Makwana Realtors. With a stronger team, a wider network, and an unwavering commitment to excellence, Agrawal & Makwana Realtors became a one-stop solution for real estate marketing and investment opportunities. The firm expanded its reach, helping even more families turn their dreams of homeownership into reality.

Our Trusted Real Estate
2025

Property Samadhan

As the world embraced digital solutions, Aman Agrawal, Kapil’s son, took the next step in the firm’s journey. Property Samadhan by Agrawal & Makwana Realtors was launched—an online platform dedicated to real estate solutions at your fingertips. With three generations of experience, 40 years of trade knowledge, a reputation built on trust, and a vision for the future, Agrawal & Makwana Realtors continues to evolve, ensuring that your dream home is always within reach. Thank you for being part of our journey—we look forward to serving you for years to come!
