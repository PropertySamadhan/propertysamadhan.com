Golf Manor

Kanadia, Indore

Description

Silver Golf Manor is a luxurious residential project located in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India. Developed by the renowned M. Jhaveri Group, this exclusive community offers premium plots with a focus on elegant living and personalized design. One of the standout features of Silver Golf Manor is its unique approach to plot demarcation. Each plot comes with theme-based boundary walls and distinctive entry gates, allowing residents to express their individual style and create a truly personalized living space. This attention to detail sets Silver Golf Manor apart, creating a sense of exclusivity and sophistication. The project boasts meticulously crafted landscapes designed by One Landscape, a leading name in international landscape architecture. Their expertise has transformed Silver Golf Manor into a visual masterpiece, where lush greenery and artistic elements blend seamlessly to create a serene and inviting atmosphere. The artistic touches are further enhanced by the Office of Kalhan, whose innovative designs contribute to the project's overall aesthetic appeal. Residents of Silver Golf Manor can enjoy a range of amenities designed to enhance their lifestyle. These include a turf court for sports enthusiasts, an amphitheater for community events and gatherings, a green avenue for leisurely strolls, and a dedicated kids' play area for the younger residents. With its focus on personalized design, lush landscapes, and a host of amenities, Silver Golf Manor offers a truly exceptional living experience in Indore.
